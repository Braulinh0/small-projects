Ingredients:
2 large, ripe, round tomatoes
1 medium cucumber
1 small red onion
1 green bell pepper
150g feta cheese
1/2 cup Kalamata olives
4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 teaspoon dried oregano and salt

Step-by-Step Preparation:
1 - Wash all the vegetables well and cut the tomatoes into medium-sized pieces or wedges.
2 - Partially peel the cucumber (leaving strips of skin) and cut it into thick slices.
3 - Cut the red onion into very thin rings and the bell pepper into strips or cubes.
4 - Place all the vegetables in a large bowl along with the whole olives.
5 - Place the whole block of feta cheese on top of the vegetables or cut it into large cubes.
6 - Drizzle generously with olive oil and sprinkle with dried oregano.
7 - Add salt to taste only to the vegetables (the cheese is already salty) and serve.
