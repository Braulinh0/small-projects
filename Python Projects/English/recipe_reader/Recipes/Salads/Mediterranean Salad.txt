Ingredients
2 round tomatoes or 1 cup cherry tomatoes
1 medium cucumber, peeled and diced
1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
1 red or yellow bell pepper, chopped
1 cup cooked and drained chickpeas
1/2 cup black or green olives
100g goat cheese or mozzarella, cubed
1 handful of chopped fresh parsley
3 tablespoons olive oil and lemon juice
1 pinch of salt and black pepper

Step-by-Step Preparation:
1 - Wash all the vegetables well and cut the tomatoes into uniform pieces
2 - Cut the cucumber and bell pepper into cubes similar in size to the chickpeas
3 - Place the cooked chickpeas in a large bowl along with the rest of the vegetables
4 - Add the red onion and pitted olives
5 - Stir in the cheese of your choice and the chopped fresh parsley
6 - Prepare the dressing by mixing the olive oil with the lemon juice and Salt
7 - Pour the dressing over the salad and mix gently
8 - Serve chilled to best enjoy the flavors
