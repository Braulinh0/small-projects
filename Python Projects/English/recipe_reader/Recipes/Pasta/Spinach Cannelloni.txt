Ingredients
12 Pancakes or cannelloni pasta sheets
2 bunches of fresh spinach or 2 packages of frozen spinach
500g well-drained ricotta cheese
100g grated Parmesan cheese
1 large onion, finely chopped
1 clove of garlic, minced
1 cup white sauce (béchamel)
2 cups tomato sauce (tuco)
Pinch of nutmeg, salt, and pepper

Step-by-Step Preparation:
1 - Wash the spinach and steam it or cook it in a pan with just a little water.
2 - Drain the spinach very well to remove all the liquid and chop it finely.
3 - Sauté the onion and garlic in a pan with a drizzle of oil until golden brown.
4 - In a bowl, mix the spinach with the onion, ricotta, and half of the grated cheese.
5 - Season the mixture with salt, pepper, and a generous amount of nutmeg.
6 - Fill each pancake with two tablespoons of the mixture and roll it up. Caution
7 - Place a little tomato sauce on the base of a baking dish
8 - Arrange the cannelloni side by side and cover with the remaining sauce
