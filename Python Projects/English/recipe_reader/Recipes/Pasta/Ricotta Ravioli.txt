Ingredients
500g fresh pasta dough or pasta discs
400g well-drained ricotta cheese
100g grated cheese (Parmesan or similar)
1 large egg
1 handful of chopped walnuts
1 pinch of nutmeg, salt, and pepper
2 cups tomato sauce or tomato paste
1 drizzle of olive oil

Step-by-Step Preparation:
1 - Place the ricotta in a bowl and mix with the egg to combine.
2 - Add the grated cheese and chopped walnuts to give the filling more texture.
3 - Season with salt, pepper, and a good pinch of nutmeg.
4 - Roll out the pasta dough on a clean, dry surface.
5 - Place small balls of filling about three centimeters apart.
6 - Cover with another layer of dough and press with your fingers to remove any air.
7 - Cut the ravioli with a cutter or knife and seal the edges well.
8 - Cook in plenty of boiling salted water. until they rise to the surface
9 - Remove with a slotted spoon and transfer directly to a serving dish with hot sauce
10 - Serve with more grated cheese on top to finish the dish
