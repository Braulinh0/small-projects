Ingredients
1 flank steak (approx. 1 to 1.5 kg).
1 liter of milk, 2 cloves of garlic, and a bay leaf.
200 ml of tomato sauce, 300 g of mozzarella cheese, olives, and oregano.

Step-by-Step Preparation:
1 - Tenderizing (The Secret): Boil the flank steak in a pot with the milk, garlic, and bay leaf for 40 to 60 minutes. This ensures it doesn't become chewy. Drain and dry well.
2 - Initial Browning: Place the flank steak on a baking sheet (or grill) with the fat side up. Cook over high heat until the fat is golden brown and crispy.
3 - Assembly: Turn the meat over (fat side down). Spread the surface with tomato sauce and cover with mozzarella cheese.
4 - Broil: Place in a hot oven or cover the grill until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
5 - Finish: Remove from the oven, sprinkle with oregano, drizzle with olive oil, and add the olives.
