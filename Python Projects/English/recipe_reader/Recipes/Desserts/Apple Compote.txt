Ingredients
4 large apples (green or red)
1/2 cup white or muscovado sugar
1 cinnamon stick
1 splash of lemon juice
1 cup mineral water
1 pinch of vanilla extract

Step-by-Step Preparation:
1 - Wash the fruit well to remove any impurities.
2 - Peel the apples and carefully remove the core, being careful not to cut yourself.
3 - Cut the fruit into medium cubes or small, even slices.
4 - Place the pieces in a pot along with the sugar and cinnamon stick.
5 - Add the water and the splash of lemon juice to prevent oxidation.
6 - Cook over medium heat until it comes to a boil with the pot covered.
7 - Reduce the heat to low and let it cook for about fifteen to twenty minutes.
8 - Stir occasionally with a wooden spoon to prevent sticking.
9 - Remove from the heat when the apples are tender and the The liquid should resemble syrup.
10 - Let it cool to room temperature and then store it in the refrigerator.
