Ingredients
250g self-rising flour
125g cold butter, cubed
100g white sugar
1 large egg
300g fresh or frozen raspberries
200ml heavy cream
100g firm cream cheese
1 pinch of vanilla extract

Step-by-Step Preparation:
1 - Place the flour and sugar in a bowl to start the dough.
2 - Add the cold butter and rub it in with your fingertips until it resembles fine sand.
3 - Add the egg and combine the ingredients without overworking the mixture.
4 - Roll out the dough in a tart tin and refrigerate for half an hour.
5 - Bake the crust in a moderate oven until golden brown and crisp.
6 - Beat the heavy cream with a little sugar and the cream cheese in a bowl.
7 - Add the vanilla extract to give the filling a richer flavor.
8 - Fill the cooled tart crust with the smooth cream. 9 - Arrange the raspberries one by one for a beautiful presentation.
10 - Refrigerate for two hours before serving to allow it to firm up.
